CLASSIC

Guessed Letters:

VICTORY

The guesser won by guessing all the letters!

GAME OVER

The guesser lost by guessing wrong too many times!

HOW TO PLAY
This is a two-player game, grab a friend!
1
Tell your friend to look away. Then, type your secret text in the first text box and click "Done". The following placeholders will appear:
_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _
2
Your friend should try to figure out what you typed by guessing random letters. To guess a letter, type the letter in the second box and click "Guess".
Guess
3
If your friend guesses all the letters in your secret text, they win! However, if they guess wrong too many times, they lose!
